# Research Notes: Booking and Quote Management Best Practices

## Booking UX Best Practices (from Ralabs)

### Key Principles:
1. **Clear First Step** - CTA above the fold, sticky buttons, progress indicators
2. **Availability Visibility** - Gray out unavailable slots, show tooltips, suggest alternatives
3. **Quality Content** - High-quality images, benefit-focused descriptions, social proof
4. **Smart Defaults** - Pre-selected options, "Best Value" labels, recommended highlights
5. **Transparent Pricing** - Show full price early, expandable price breakdown, clear refund policies
6. **Mobile-First Design** - Sticky CTAs, larger touch targets, fast page loads
7. **Error Recovery** - Inline validation, edit-friendly flows, specific error messages
8. **Accessibility** - Alt text, keyboard support, readable contrast, inclusive filters
9. **Continuous Iteration** - Funnel tracking, A/B tests, usability testing

## Essential Features for Booking Systems:

### From Industry Research:
- Automated email notifications and booking reminders
- Calendar integration and availability management
- Dynamic pricing based on demand/seasonality
- Mobile responsiveness
- Payment processing integration
- Customer relationship management
- Reporting and analytics
- Contract/document management
- Multi-channel communication

### DJ/Event Booking Specific:
- Event planning forms
- Request lists and timelines
- Contract signing (e-signatures)
- Client data storage
- Automated scheduling to prevent double-booking
- Lead capture and follow-up
- Payment collection
- Survey/feedback collection
